# Full Timeline of the Fractured Isles

#history #timeline

> *"The history of the Fractured Isles is long enough that no single account can be fully trusted. What follows is the consensus of the Brass Dragon Grand Library, the Arcane Senate's historical faculty, and several centuries of Ebony Order records — the closest thing to an authoritative history the Isles possess, which is to say: a best guess, documented with admirable rigour and occasional significant error."*

**Date systems:**
- **AB** — *After Betrayal.* Counts from the Great Betrayal and the imprisonment of the Outer Gods.
- **AF** — *After Fracturing.* Counts from the cataclysm that ended the Elven Empire.
- The two systems do not convert directly. The gap between them spans most of the world's inhabited history.

---

## ⬛ Before Time — The Primordial Era

| Event | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **The Primordial War** | The [[cosmology/The Outer Gods\|Outer Gods]] war in the formless dark before creation. The universe takes shape from their conflict. |
| **The Primordial Crucible builds the world** | The most powerful Outer God constructs the material plane and spends millennia preserving its balance. |
| **The Eioran arise** | The first mortal race. Trusted allies of the Crucible. |
| **The Great Betrayal *(Mayra Proddare)*** | The Eioran imprison the other Outer Gods using stolen divine power, then shatter the Crucible itself out of fear. The Crucible's last act: creating the [[cosmology/The Marred\|Marred]]. The Eioran's guilt transforms them into the [[cosmology/The New Gods\|New Gods]]. This moment becomes **0 AB**. |

## 🟤 Age of Foundations — 0 to 1500 AB

*[[Age of Foundations\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **0 AB** | **The Great Betrayal** — Outer Gods imprisoned, Crucible shattered, New Gods ascend from dissolved Eioran. |
| **0–200 AB** | The three **proto-races** emerge: dragons (inheriting Eioran arcane knowledge), giants (physical scale and strength), elves (organisational genius). |
| **200–600 AB** | Dragons begin arcane repair of the shattered plane. Giants reshape geography and cut the first roads. Elves begin agriculture and cataloguing. |
| **600–900 AB** | **Younger races emerge:** dwarves from deep rock; gnomes near old war forges; tieflings and changelings near planar scars; orcs from order-chaos collision zones; humans and halflings from recovering forests; aarakocra from the high air; genasi from elemental hotspots. |
| **Throughout** | The [[cosmology/The Marred\|Marred]] appear. Not understood as what they are. First encounters shape communities in ways still legible in modern cultures. |

## 🟠 Age of Law — 5200 to 8300 AB

*[[Age of Law\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **5200 AB** | **The Draconic Infraction begins** — two-hundred-year war of duels and assassinations over whether younger races should access arcane magic. Battlefields still magically tainted today. |
| **c.5350 AB** | The elder dragon **Aravoss** wraps their body around a mountain range and petrifies to seal the most dangerous magics unleashed in the war. *([[locations/Landmark Locations#The Frozen Wing\|The Frozen Wing]])* Informal Concord agreements end active conflict. |
| **c.5400 AB** | The Draconic Infraction ends. |
| **6100 AB** | ⭐ **The Concord of Seven** — arcane schools established, magical law formalised, magic shared with all races under oversight. The document that created the modern world. |
| **7000 AB** | **The Ferrite Rebellion** — dwarven civil war over tradition vs. innovation, fought in underground fortresses with rune engines and siege machinery. |
| **c.7100 AB** | The **Forged Accords** end the Rebellion. Dwarven culture shaped into its characteristic balance of deep conservatism and fierce innovation. |
| **7600 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Korden\|Saint Korden]] — dwarven heir who chose justice over inheritance. |
| **7900 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Aestri\|Saint Aestri]] — aarakocra who maps the entire Fractured Isles in a single lifetime. |
| **8200 AB** | **The Arcane Uprising** — Cadrin, Tothiri's greatest student, attempts to seize forbidden magics. Fails catastrophically. The prison built to contain dangerous knowledge becomes the **Ebony Order's first detention facility**. Cadrin its first prisoner. |
| **8300 AB** | ⭐ **Foundation of the Ebony Order** by [[faith/Saints#Saint Tothiri\|Saint Tothiri]]. |
| **8350 AB** | **Foundation of the Arcane Senate** — civilian oversight counterpart to the Order. |
| **8450 AB** | **The Crystal Plague** — magical disease from Infraction residue. Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Merrow\|Saint Merrow]], who treats the sick as their own body crystallises. [[cosmology/The New Gods#Vapos — The Cleansing Tide\|Vapos]] descends to grant sainthood — the only recorded instance of a New God taking direct physical action in the material plane. |

## 🟢 Age of Saints — 8300 to 9800 AB

*[[Age of Saints\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **c.8300 AB** | Institutional stability established. The large catastrophes give way to smaller ones, each managed by the frameworks the previous ones prompted. |
| **9100 AB** | **The Age of Prophecy** — the **Veiled Prophet** rises, a changeling who predicts disasters before they occur and prevents several. The question of preemptive enforcement becomes too pointed to ignore. The Prophet disappears. [[organisations/The Chromatic Inquisition\|Chromatic Inquisition]] develops seer protocols in response. |
| **9200 AB** | [[faith/Saints#Saint Ysolde\|Saint Ysolde]] completes their final painting — five figures facing an elemental catastrophe, titled *The End of All* — and **disappears**. No body found. Painting classified at the highest level in the Brass Library. |
| **c.9200 AB** | **Purification of the Garden begins** — wood elves start the 412-year work of cleansing planar corruption from what will become [[locations/Landmark Locations#The Purified Garden\|The Purified Garden]]. |
| **9600 AB** | **Purification of the Garden completed** after four hundred and twelve years of continuous effort across multiple generations. |
| **9650 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Varelis\|Saint Varelis]] — wood elf who led their house out of the elven empire and spent their life teaching farming to displaced communities. |
| **9700 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Zaivine\|Saint Zaivine]] — tiefling bard and painter who spent a lifetime noticing who was suffering. |
| **9750 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Farion\|Saint Farion]] — deep gnome who united feuding factions through diplomacy and the demonstration that creation beats competition. |
| **9880 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Loujar\|Saint Loujar]] — orc saint who spent decades demonstrating what orc civilisation actually was. |
| **9900 AB** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Tothiri\|Saint Tothiri]] — human scholar, founder of the Ebony Order, patron of knowledge and secrets. |

## 🔴 Age of Empire — 9800 AB to 1 AF

*[[Age of Empire\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **9800 AB** | ⭐ **The Weeping Crusade begins** — twenty-year religious war between followers of [[cosmology/The New Gods#Moran — The Ceaseless Mother\|Moran]] and [[cosmology/The New Gods#Kindra — The Fervent Flame\|Kindra]] over the nature of the afterlife. |
| **c.9820 AB** | **Eloris** — arms dealer supplying both sides of the Crusade — witnesses the results of their trade at close range. Begins their transformation. |
| **c.9850 AB** | Weeping Crusade ends. Eloris credited with disarming both sides. Later becomes [[faith/Saints#Saint Eloris\|Saint Eloris]], the most widely venerated saint in the Isles. |
| **9950 AB** | Rise of **Celeborn** — elven kingdom begins formal conquest, framing it as solving the problem of the younger races' chaos. Two houses dissent: Celzala tends the wounded; Varelis feeds the displaced. |
| **9995 AB** | **The Arcane Engine completed** — designed to broadcast submission to all minds simultaneously. Goes operational. |
| **1 AF** | ⭐ **THE GREAT FRACTURING** — the Arcane Engine fails under the pressure of the power it channels. The material plane shatters. Rifts open across the world, pouring elemental creatures in from every plane simultaneously. The elven empire collapses. |

## ⚫ Age of Ruin — 1 to 300 AF

*[[Age of Ruin\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **1 AF** | The material plane is unrecognisable. The asteroid belt forms from rock and debris thrown into the upper atmosphere. Elder races begin the devastating work of sealing rifts. |
| **10 AF** | Life of [[faith/Saints#Saint Celzala\|Saint Celzala]] — their house becomes the **dark elves**, their silver curse shaped into beauty by the gods in recognition of their service to the dying of every race. |
| **25 AF** | [[faith/Saints#Saint Bricwar\|Saint Bricwar]] — giant king — **falls in the final day of rift-sealing**. Granted sainthood. Their glowing scars pass to descendants; Grand Chieftain Solanna Stonewalker bears them today. |
| **50 AF** | The dark elf community established under Celzala's house. |
| **100 AF** | ⭐ **Foundation of the Undead Dominion** — grand liches, vampires, and wraith-lords negotiate their place in the new world. |
| **150 AF** | The **Four Ordos** (Houses) of the Undead Lands formally constituted: Sanguinium, Ossis, Carnis, Spiritus. |
| **c.175 AF** | Rise of **Emperor Vauldris the Golden** — unites surviving human settlements through charisma and the use of enslaved labour from other races. Brings humanity back from the edge of extinction. Methods remain a source of diplomatic tension to this day. |
| **c.250 AF** | **Fall of the First Human Empire** — multi-racial coalition overthrow. First genuinely representative human governing structures established. |
| **300 AF** | End of major rift-sealing work. The world begins, slowly, to stabilise. |

## 🔵 Age of Recovery — 300 to 1800 AF

*[[Age of Recovery\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **300 AF** | Dragon provinces consolidate. Ebony Order and Arcane Senate begin rebuilding from wartime skeleton operations. |
| **c.400 AF** | **The Spirit Riots** — living residents of the Undead Lands protest undead labour ethics. Resolved through negotiation; formal covenant ratified. |
| **c.500 AF** | ⭐ **The TideLock Agreement** — Vapos's clergy mediate an eleven-year negotiation that stabilises the southern shipping lanes. Holds to the present day. |
| **c.600 AF** | Life of **Alborak SteelFist** — dwarven figure whose defeat of a dragon in single combat prompted the first formal dwarven-dragon trade negotiations. *(Every detail of the fight is disputed except the outcome.)* |
| **c.700 AF** | Life of **Corvin Black Feather** — aarakocra explorer completes the first fully documented circumnavigation, proving the world is spherical. |
| **c.800 AF** | **The Frozen Famine** strikes the [[nations/Silver Dragon Province\|Silver Dragon Province]]. Green Province druids arrive with food and medicine without being asked. The alliance that follows is the most stable in the Isles. |
| **c.850 AF** | **The Relaxation Rebellion** — hot spring workers of the [[nations/Gold Dragon Province\|Gold Dragon Province]] strike. Resolved by mediation; produces worker protections now foundational to labour law across three provinces. |
| **c.900 AF** | **The Ashen Eruption** — [[nations/Red Dragon Province\|Red Dragon Province]] volcanic disaster caused by mining operations that had been warned against for decades. Reconstruction produces the province's modern disaster-response expertise. |
| **c.1200 AF** | Life of **Captain Seraph Tideborn** — first to survive a close approach to the [[locations/Landmark Locations#The Sunken Maw\|Sunken Maw]] and return. Maps still primary reference. |
| **c.500 AF–present** | The **Wayfinder** — a recurring changeling figure appearing at crossroads at moments of crisis, pointing the lost toward safety. One person, many people, or something else entirely. |
| **1800 AF** | ⭐ **Foundation of the [[organisations/Pathfinders of Aestri\|Pathfinders of Aestri]]**, named in honour of Saint Aestri. |

## 🟣 The Modern Age — 1800 AF to Present

*[[The Modern Age\|→ Full page]]*

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **1800 AF** | Pathfinders founded. Begin systematic Vault tracking and world mapping. |
| **c.2000 AF** | Life of **Elyrian Voss** — first person to enter an [[locations/Landmark Locations#The Eioran Vaults\|Eioran Vault]] and return. Account remains the primary source for Vault interior research. |
| **c.2200 AF** | ⚠️ **Vault appearances begin increasing in frequency** and appearing in locations that match no previous pattern. Pathfinders report to the Ebony Order. Order acknowledges receipt. |
| **c.2400 AF** | Life of **Solenne Asp (The Lost Mage)** — halfling who ascends to the asteroid belt via experimental magic. Returns changed. Refuses to say what they saw. Paints a series of works since classified by the Brass Library. |
| **c.2500 AF** | ⚠️ **Marred cult resurgence** — all four Marred see increased cult activity that the Order's modelling cannot fully explain. Standard drivers present but insufficient to account for the scale. |
| **c.2600 AF** | Life of **Gariss Thorn** — Ebony Order's most celebrated field agent and most-studied cautionary example. The Purge and the Honourable End; three ongoing ethics reviews. |
| **c.2750 AF** | ⚠️ **Greylands disturbances recorded** — signals described by Order specialists as "disturbances" and, more candidly, as warnings. Nature of what Moran is warning about not disclosed. |
| **c.2900 AF** | ⚠️ **Outer God prison degradation** — prisons designed to be permanent show structural weakening that should not be possible. No mechanism identified. |
| **2990 AF** | **Major prophecies re-emerge** across multiple traditions, many traceable to Ysolde's followers. Speak of five figures, an elemental convergence, a choice that cannot be undone. |
| **⭐ 3000 AF** | **PRESENT DAY.** The Fractured Isles are stable, beautiful, and quietly beginning to run out of time. |
