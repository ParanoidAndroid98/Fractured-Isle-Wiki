# Silver Dragon Province

#nations #dragon-province #silver-province

> *"Endurance is not survival. Endurance is surviving in a way that maintains your integrity. The cold does not care about your feelings; the community does."*

**Provincial Dragon:** Ancient Silver Dragon **Frezant**  
**Capital:** Fengari Peaks  
**Dominant Faith:** [[cosmology/The New Gods#Theran|Theran]]  
**Economy:** Community investment, the Vault of Genus scientific collection, coastal trade

---

## Overview

The furthest south of all the provinces — frozen extremities of the continent where winters are long and summers brief. The people are, once you have earned a place among them, **extraordinarily loyal and generous**. The process of earning that place is understood to take time and to require demonstration rather than assertion.

The province has the smallest cities and the **largest per-capita community investment** of any dragon province. When the fish fail or the crops are thin, the province does not divide along lines of who has most; it pools what it has. This has kept it alive through conditions that would have destroyed a more individually-oriented culture, and the people know it.

---

## History

The **Frozen Famine** (~800 AF) remains the defining event of the province's self-understanding — not because it was the worst thing that happened here, but because of what it revealed about character and neighbours'. The [[nations/Green Dragon Province|Green Dragon Province]] arrived with food and medicine without being asked. The relationship that built between those two provinces has been one of the most stable alliances in the Isles ever since — based not on strategic calculation but on the memory of that specific generosity.

## Major Settlements

![[assets/province-silver-city.jpg]]

**Fengari Peaks** *(Capital)*  
The province's largest city, carved into and around a great mountain peak. The architecture descends into the mountain as much as it rises above it, creating an interior city that remains habitable through the worst winters.

**Glawyn Hold**  
A coastal fishing city and the province's primary trading port, where first impressions of outsiders are formed and where the province's reputation for unfriendly receptions is largely generated.

**Vhalinder**  
An inland settlement built around the **Vault of Genus** — a collection of specimens of every creature known to the Isles, maintained by the province and considered one of the most significant scientific resources in the world.

## Provincial Dragon: Frezant

![[assets/dragon-frezant.jpg]]

**Humanoid Form:** Stout, weathered, white hair and piercing white eyes. Scale-flecked skin. Braided beard with carved bone jewellery and whale ivory ornaments. Arctic clothing sewn with elaborate gold design work.

**Personality:** Slow to trust and very slow to distrust once trust is earned. Quietly observant. Has a capacity for strategic patience that younger races find difficult to distinguish from passivity.  
**Philosophy:** Endurance is not survival. Endurance is surviving in a way that maintains your integrity.  
**Greatest Achievement:** The alliance with the Green Province — not the formal treaty, but the relationship that preceded it.  
**Greatest Regret:** The three settlements lost in the winter of 2740 AF before the weather system's unnatural origin was identified. Frezant considers this a failure of pattern recognition.

**Current Plot Hooks:**
- The Vault of Genus has a new specimen that no one can identify. It was not added by any authorised staff member.
- The northern ice systems have been behaving unusually — cold currents where there should be warmth, warmth where there should be cold.

## Provincial Festival
**Winterfire** (during [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Seventh Month Silver Rest|Silver Rest]]): Entire communities gather around enormous bonfires. Storytelling, communal meals, and the singing tradition that exists nowhere else in the Isles at this scale.
