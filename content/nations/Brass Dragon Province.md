# Brass Dragon Province

#nations #dragon-province #brass-province

> *"The world is not improved by fast decisions. It is improved by correct decisions reached at whatever speed correctness requires."*

**Provincial Dragon:** Ancient Brass Dragon **Ximoal**  
**Capital:** Solvaryn  
**Economy:** Scholarship, the Grand Library, the Ebony Hold

---

## Overview

Where the world goes when it needs to know something. The libraries here contain the **most complete record of the Fractured Isles' history and knowledge that has ever been assembled**. The province's people are collectively the most educated in the Isles. They are also, its critics note, the most likely to still be researching a problem when it finishes resolving itself.

The Brass Province has **correctly diagnosed every major crisis in the Isles for the past two thousand years**. Its record on acting on those diagnoses in time is more mixed.

---

## Government

An **academic senate** — elected scholars who administer provincial functions between sessions of the full assembly. Ximoal chairs the assembly and holds final authority on military and treaty matters. The system produces government that is deliberate, extensively documented, and very slow to respond to anything not anticipated by the planning process.

![[assets/province-brass-city.jpg]]

**Solvaryn** *(Capital — "The City of Scholars")*  
Home to the Grand Library, the Zephralis Institute, and the Ebony Hold. Population ~240,000, with a significant transient academic population on top.

**Cindrathin Citadel**  
A fortified research city handling the province's most sensitive archival work. The public section is a significant tourist attraction. The restricted sections are not.

## Provincial Dragon: Ximoal

![[assets/dragon-ximoal.jpg]]

**Humanoid Form:** Tall, metallic-looking older woman, patches of brass scales visible on hands and neck, long arcane robes, long slightly-clawed fingers. Purple draconic eyes behind round spectacles. Usually has a book.

**Personality:** Patient almost to a fault, genuinely respectful of other perspectives, very difficult to surprise, and quietly tracking more variables than anyone in the room knows about.  
**Greatest Achievement:** The Grand Library — specifically, the decision to make the majority of it publicly accessible.  
**Greatest Regret:** The three times the province correctly identified an emerging crisis, presented its findings to the relevant authorities, and watched the crisis unfold anyway while those authorities deliberated.

**Current Plot Hooks:**
- Ximoal has classified a section of the library that was previously open.
- The Order's Ebony Hold has received an unusual volume of visitors from outside the province.
- A graduate student has found a text in the deep archive they should not have been able to access.

**Festival of Questions** (during [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Sixth Month Dreamfall|Dreamfall]]): Children are permitted to ask scholars anything. Public philosophical debates run continuously. The most intellectually stimulating provincial festival by general consensus.
