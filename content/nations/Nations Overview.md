# Nations & Peoples

#nations

The world was divided among the ancient races in the aftermath of the Fracturing. What emerged were not empires but covenants — territories shaped as much by their ruling personalities as by geography. Living under a dragon ruler is not the same as living under a mortal monarch. The ancient dragons do not fear death in the way a human king fears it, which removes one of the primary engines of short-term political thinking.

## The Dragon Provinces

Nine provinces, each governed by one of the great ancient dragons, all bound by the [[history/History Overview#6100 AB|Concord]] that prevents any one of them from doing to the others what the elves once tried to do to everyone.
