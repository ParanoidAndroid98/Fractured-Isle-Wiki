# The Undead Lands

#nations #undead-lands

> *"Is it safe?" The honest answer is that it is among the safest places in the Fractured Isles for the living, for the straightforward reason that the undead who run it have a vested interest in its reputation, its economy, and the continued willingness of living people to come here, stay here, and ultimately become part of its workforce.*

---

## Overview

![[assets/undead-lands-intro.jpg]]

The Undead Lands are not a blighted wasteland ruled by cackling necromancers. They are a set of **sophisticated city-states with functioning economies, genuine civic culture**, and a covenant with their living residents that most people, given the full details, find more reasonable than they expected.

---

## The Covenant

At its core, the covenant is an exchange:

**What the living receive:** Freedom from manual labour (the work of the cities is done by the undead), a high standard of living, and the resources to spend their lives pursuing whatever they choose.

**What the living agree to:** Upon their natural death, their remains are available to the undead administrative structure for whatever purpose the relevant Ordo determines.

This agreement is entered into consciously and with legal representation provided by the undead houses free of charge. It is not coercive. It is also, some observers note, entered into by people who are not yet dead and therefore find it **somewhat abstract**.

The living residents of the Undead Lands have, by measurable outcomes, among the **highest quality of life** of any population in the Isles.

## The Four Ordos

### Ordo Sanguinium

![[assets/undead-ordo-sanguinium.jpg]] — House of Blood

**Ruler:** Grand Countess **Vitalis Sanguinis**  
**Speciality:** Transformation magic, blood study, hypnotic and persuasive arts  
**Relationship with Living:** Close and carefully maintained; employs living staff directly

The vampire population of the Undead Lands, governing the aspects of civic life under their expertise. The most engaged with living culture of the four ordos — vampires require the living in a way that liches and wraiths do not.

Grand Countess Vitalis Sanguinis is imposing in the technical sense — nine to ten feet tall, of enormous personal presence, with the kind of authority that fills rooms without announcing itself. She is also genuinely reasonable. She wants the covenant to work, and it has served her interests and those of the living residents well for over two thousand years.

---

### Ordo Ossis

![[assets/undead-lich-queen.jpg]] — House of Bone

**Ruler:** The Lich Queen *(personal name not publicly used)*  
**Speciality:** Necromancy, undead labour administration, bone artistry  
**Relationship with Living:** Administrative and respectful

The Lich Queen is the **oldest continuously-serving head of government in the Fractured Isles**, having occupied her position since the Dominion's founding. The House of Bone is the most artistically accomplished of the four ordos — the necromantic artistry that emerged from the lich tradition has produced some of the most significant works in the Isles. The great ossuaries maintained by the ordo are, by the consensus of every art critic who has visited, extraordinary.

---

### Ordo Carnis

![[assets/undead-ordo-carnis.jpg]] — House of Rot

**Ruler:** Archmagus **Putris Khar**  
**Speciality:** Disease research, viral study, medical advancement through self-experimentation  
**Relationship with Living:** Caring and medically focused

The ordo that the [[nations/Black Dragon Province|Black Dragon Province]] has its most direct relationship with, and the source of the most significant advances in disease medicine in the Isles. The zombified undead of the ordo are uniquely positioned to study infectious and magical diseases — they cannot be harmed by most of what they study, and their own slow biological processes have given them an intuitive understanding of decomposition and regeneration.

Archmagus Putris Khar is not what people expect. The rotting frame, the flowers and fungi sprouting from every surface, the deliberate cultivation of new life from decay — all intentional and philosophical. Khar is one of the most **thoughtful and gentle** personalities in the Undead Lands leadership.

---

### Ordo Spiritus

![[assets/undead-ordo-spiritus.jpg]] — House of Soul

**Ruler:** Master **Vaelith Mournglass**  
**Speciality:** Espionage, planar communication, management of the Greylands boundary  
**Relationship with Living:** Somewhat distant by necessity

The wraith and banshee population — incorporeal undead whose existence bridges the material world and the Greylands. This position gives them capabilities the other houses cannot match:
- Wraiths can move through material obstacles
- Banshees can communicate across distances physical messengers cannot cover
- Master Vaelith Mournglass maintains an information network touching every significant institution in the Isles

The restraint with which the ordo exercises its capabilities is either genuine principle or very sophisticated long-term strategy. Vaelith has never been moved to clarify which.
