## Setting at a Glance

**Current Date:** 3000 AF  
**Calendar:** 8 months of 45 days + 5 Days of Fracture  
**Magic System:** Governed by the [[organisations/The Arcane Senate|Arcane Senate]], enforced by the [[organisations/The Ebony Order|Ebony Order]]  
**Governing Dates:** AB = After Betrayal · AF = After Fracturing

### Core Themes
- **The Cost of Certainty** — Nearly every catastrophe was caused by someone absolutely sure they were right.
- **Immortality and Its Weight** — The ancient races are still here, and long life grants perspective, not wisdom.
- **Faith Without Certainty** — The gods exist and are demonstrable; what they want is not.
- **Mortal Agency** — The Outer Gods are imprisoned. The world has been handed back to its inhabitants.

### What Makes This Setting Unique
- The world is **governed by immortals who are not gods** — dragons, liches, and giants with real philosophies and real vulnerabilities.
- The **elves carry a curse that is also a gift** — their bodies are slowly consumed by metal (gold, silver, or bronze depending on lineage).
- **The dead have their own nation**, and it functions as a sophisticated set of city-states.
- **Magic has a history and a politics** — to be a spellcaster is to operate within a system.
