## Purpose

Magic was now widely distributed. The [[history/History Overview#6100 AB|Concord of Seven]] had established what was permitted. The question that remained: **who dealt with what was not permitted**, and with the knowledge and artifacts too dangerous to permit but too significant to destroy?

The Ebony Order was the answer. Founded over ten thousand years ago, it is by a considerable margin **the oldest functioning institution in the Fractured Isles**. In that time it has been feared, respected, reformed, corrupted, investigated, and reformed again. Its current form is the result of seventeen major internal reviews and at least three external crises of public confidence.

---

## Members & Operations

Members are trained as a **hybrid of paladin and scholar**:
- In library facilities: simple black robes with gold or silver trim, comfortable shoes, reading glasses where needed.
- In detention facilities and field operations: **black and gold plate armour** — the most recognisable uniform in the Isles.
- Every member is trained in both arcane security and physical combat, specifically to handle entities that magic alone or physical force alone cannot contain.

---

## The Libraries

The libraries are **architectural achievements**:
- **Public sections:** Genuinely open to all.
- **Restricted sections:** Require demonstrated need and significant vetting.
- **Deep archives:** Contain artifacts and knowledge considered too dangerous for any access. Protected by architectural labyrinths, arcane suppression fields, and — in several facilities — things that have been sealed in so that nothing can be sealed out.

---

## The Detention Facilities

The Order imprisons beings who cannot safely exist in the world — whether from planar corruption, uncontrollable magical ability, or crimes no conventional prison could contain. By the Order's own accounting, conditions are humane. **Sentence lengths are indefinite.** Several inmates have been inside since before the current dragon rulers were hatched.

Whether this is justice, necessity, or something else is a question the Order's own ethics committee has been debating since 8800 AB without resolution.

Notable prisoner: **Cadrin** — the half-giant archmage who attempted to seize forbidden magics in the Arcane Uprising of 8200 AB. The first prisoner. Reportedly still there.

---

## Current Activities (3000 AF)

The Order has been tracking something it has not yet made public:

- **2200 AF:** Eioran Vaults appearing with increasing frequency and in locations matching no previous pattern.
- **2500 AF:** Marred cults in resurgence that the Order's modelling cannot fully explain.
- **2750 AF:** Greylands sending signals their specialists describe as "disturbances" — or, more candidly, "warnings."
- **2900 AF:** The prisons holding the Outer Gods showing signs of structural weakening that should not be possible.

## Relationship with Other Organisations

- **[[The Chromatic Inquisition]]:** Close allies, often confused for the same organisation. The Order handles knowledge and artifacts; the Inquisition handles people and power.
- **[[The Arcane Senate]]:** The defining institutional tension of the magical world. The Order believes safety margins should be more conservative than the Senate tends to think.
- **[[Pathfinders of Aestri]]:** The Pathfinders report Vault anomalies to the Order. The Order acknowledges receipt. Neither has explained what they intend to do.
