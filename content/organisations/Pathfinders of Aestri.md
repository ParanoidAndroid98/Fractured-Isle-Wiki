---

## Purpose

Founded in 1800 AF in explicit homage to [[faith/Saints#Saint Aestri|Saint Aestri]], the Pathfinders are the Isles' primary **exploration and cartographic organisation**. They maintain:
- The most comprehensive maps of the known world
- The most complete record of [[locations/The Eioran Vaults|Vault appearances and disappearances]]
- The largest collection of traveller accounts, road conditions, and geographical surveys outside the Brass Dragon Grand Library

---

## Membership

Membership is open to **anyone who completes a minimum journey of three thousand miles** and submits a documented account of what they observed. The organisation is deliberately informal in structure, funded by a combination of provincial grants, membership dues, and the sale of maps and travel guides.

**Political neutrality** is a core institutional value, maintained consistently enough over 1,200 years that even the Inquisition generally takes their geographic records at face value.

---

## Vault Tracking

In the modern age, the Pathfinders' Vault tracking function has become their most sensitive work. The increasing frequency of appearances since 2200 AF, and the pattern anomalies beginning around 2750 AF, have been reported to both the [[The Ebony Order|Ebony Order]] and the [[The Arcane Senate|Arcane Senate]].

Both institutions have acknowledged receipt of the reports. **Neither has explained what they intend to do with them.**
