# The Arcane Senate

#organisations #arcane-senate

**Founded:** 8350 AB  
**Headquarters:** Avioln Arcan, [[nations/Bronze Dragon Province|Bronze Dragon Province]]  
**Current Presiding Senator:** High Senator **Draveth Caul** (Bronze Dragon Province delegate)

---

## Purpose

Founded to govern the distribution and regulation of magic in the material world — specifically to implement the framework the [[history/History Overview#6100 AB|Concord of Seven]] established and to serve as the **civilian counterpart** to the [[The Ebony Order|Ebony Order's]] enforcement function. Where the Order deals with what magic must not be allowed to do, the Senate deals with **what magic is permitted and encouraged to do**, and who gets to make those decisions.

---

## Structure

The Senate operates from the Bronze Dragon Province, maintaining delegates in every dragon province and in larger independent territories. Membership includes:
- Representatives from every arcane school
- Delegates from the dragon provinces
- A rotating seat held by independent practitioners

Proceedings are **public**. Debates are fierce. Policy outputs are **binding on every licensed practitioner in the Isles**.

---

## The Senate vs. the Ebony Order

The relationship between the Senate and the Ebony Order is **the defining institutional tension of the magical world**:
- The Senate believes knowledge should flow as freely as safety structures allow.
- The Order believes safety structures should be more conservative than the Senate tends to think.

Both positions have been validated and catastrophically wrong at different points in history. The current balance is the product of those experiences.
