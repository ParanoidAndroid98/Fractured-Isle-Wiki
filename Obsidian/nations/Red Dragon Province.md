## Overview

Built on fire — literally, given the volcanic range at its heart, and figuratively in its culture, economy, and understanding of strength. The great fortresses and sprawling industrial cities are not monuments to luxury but to **capability**: what human hands and draconic knowledge can build when pointed in the same direction.

Outsiders often misread the martial character as aggression. It is more accurate to call it **directness**. The Red Province does not have patience for the oblique or theoretical. Problems are identified and addressed. Disputes are resolved with remarkable efficiency.

---

## History

The Red Province sits on what was, before the Fracturing, some of the most contested land in the elven campaign. The founding dragon ruler Umeial chose to lean into the persistent magical instability rather than neutralise it.

The **Ashen Eruption** (~900 AF) was the province's most significant self-inflicted wound: a volcanic system destabilised by mining operations that had been warned against and proceeded anyway. The rebuilding effort produced the Red Province's current expertise in disaster response and hazard management — knowledge shared with other provinces and credited with reducing casualties in several subsequent crises.

---

## Government

A **martial meritocracy**. Umeial governs from Emberhold Bastion, but day-to-day administration runs through a military command structure in which rank is earned rather than inherited. Civil positions — engineering, resource management, emergency response — carry equivalent rank to military positions. The distinction: between those who contribute and those who do not.

---

## Culture & Society

Strength is understood expansively: physical capability, engineering genius, the ability to lead through a crisis, the stubbornness to rebuild after disaster, and the **moral courage to tell a superior officer they are wrong**.

Innovation is celebrated here in a way even the Bronze Province doesn't quite match — because in the Red Province, innovation is **immediately applied**. New mechanisms go straight from workshop to field. Some work. Some cause the kind of spectacle the province has developed a healthy tradition of treating as educational.

---

## Military

The most technically sophisticated provincial military in the Isles, combining conventional force with engineering capability. Signature units:
- **Ashguard** — heavy infantry in volcanic-ore armour who function as both soldiers and first-response disaster workers
- **Firewatch** — aerial reconnaissance and fast-response force

## Major Settlements

![[assets/province-red-city.jpg]]

**Emberhold Bastion** *(Capital)*  
The political and industrial heart, built into the side of the largest active volcano in the Scarlet Mountains. The city's forges never go cold. Population ~200,000.

**Cindervale**  
The province's primary agricultural centre, located in the fertile ashfields downwind of the mountains. The volcanic soil produces harvests of unusual richness and unusual flavour.

**Pyrelearn**  
The province's centre of technical education and the home of the most significant engineering guild in the Isles. The campus is famously loud.

## Provincial Dragon: Umeial

![[assets/dragon-umeial.jpg]]

**Draconic Form:** A vast ancient dragon, deep crimson scales scarred by centuries of conflict, wingspan broad enough to shadow the Emberhold parade ground completely.  
**Humanoid Form:** Large muscular androgynous figure, long red hair, horns, claw-like fingers, red scales visible on the skin, scars on arms and face. Sleeveless tunic armour, heavy jewellery, multiple piercings.

**Personality:** Direct, impatient with pretension, genuinely interested in capability wherever they find it. More willing to be wrong than most dragons would admit.  
**Philosophy:** Strength is demonstrated, not claimed. The future is built, not awaited. The only dishonour is performing strength you do not have.  
**Greatest Achievement:** Reconstruction of the volcanic districts after the Ashen Eruption — completing in twelve years what engineers estimated would take forty.  
**Greatest Regret:** Approving deeper mining of the northern volcanic range that caused the Eruption. The engineer who objected was overruled.

**Current Plot Hooks:**
- Umeial has recently recalled a treaty negotiation team early with no explanation.
- Something in the northern mountains is producing readings the Firewatch cannot classify.
- The dragon has been spending considerable private time in their hoard, which the court finds unusual.
