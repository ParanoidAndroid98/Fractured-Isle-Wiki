# The New Gods

#cosmology #new-gods #religion

The New Gods are **the transformed souls of the Eioran** — the first mortal race — who gave up their physical existence to hold the world together after the [[The Outer Gods#The Great Betrayal|Great Betrayal]]. They are not omnipotent. They have domains, planes they are bound to, and a genuine interest in the welfare of mortal life. They are also complicated.

They do not speak directly or issue commandments. They communicate through **the world itself** — through the behaviour of the elements, through dreams, through the granting of [[faith/Saints|sainthood]] to those whose lives have demonstrated something worth preserving.

> *"No saint has ever been granted divinity while claiming exclusive truth."*

---

## Moran — The Ceaseless Mother

![[assets/new-god-moran.jpg]]

**Domains:** Death, rebirth, transition, the moon, safe passage  
**Symbol:** A hooded figure with many arms · a raven in flight · a silver moon  
**Sacred Creatures:** Ravens, owls, white moths  
**Plane:** The Greylands  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Seventh Month Silver Rest|Silver Rest (7th Month)]]

Goddess of death and rebirth — not death as ending, but as **the hinge between one existence and the next**. She watches over the world from the sky, embodied in the moon and the ravens said to carry souls. She rules the Greylands as a place of rest, where souls choose to remain in peace or step back through the flame for another life.

Her followers tend toward care work: healers, death-bed companions, hospice workers, midwives. The [[nations/The Elven Isle#Celesphain|dark elves]] of the House of Celzala serve her most closely, their unnatural beauty a tool for the consolation of the dying. Moran asks very little of her faithful — mostly, she asks them to be present at the difficult moments that others look away from.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Celzala|Saint Celzala]]*

## Vitasaol — The Formless Father

![[assets/new-god-vitasaol.jpg]]

**Domains:** Life, creation, evolution, the sun, possibility  
**Symbol:** A figure of scales and feathers · a sunrise · a seed mid-germination  
**Sacred Creatures:** All living things, particularly those in metamorphosis  
**Plane:** The Fey Realm / Plane of Life  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Third Month Bloomrise|Bloomrise (3rd Month)]]

God of life and creation — the counterpart to Moran, tending beginnings where Moran tends endings. Believed to be the sun itself. Shaped new life after the Great Betrayal, breathing **possibilities** into species rather than dictating what they would become.

Vitasaol's followers value **becoming over being** — life as constant transformation, stillness as a kind of small death. They can seem impatient with the resigned; they believe in the capacity of all living things to become something more.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Varelis|Saint Varelis]]*

## Theran — The Stoic King

![[assets/new-god-theran.jpg]]

**Domains:** Earth, strength, the present, unity, rebellion against repression  
**Symbol:** A clenched fist · a mountain with roots · a rough-hewn stone  
**Sacred Materials:** Stone, iron, unworked ore  
**Plane:** The Plane of Earth  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Fourth Month Stoneheart|Stoneheart (4th Month)]]

Deity of earth, stone, strength, and the present moment. Embodies the principle that **stability is not passivity** — that holding firm requires as much courage as any charge. Associated equally with peace and rebellion; Theran's strength is not the strength of conquerors but the strength of those who stand between conquerors and the people they would crush.

The dwarves are Theran's most devoted followers. The Stoic King encourages self-reliance, inner resilience, and the refusal to seek permission from the powerful before living with dignity.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Bricwar|Saint Bricwar]]*

## Ventis — The Eternal Flow

![[assets/new-god-ventis.jpg]]

**Domains:** Wind, freedom, travel, connection, spiritual unity  
**Symbol:** Two open mouths · a feather caught mid-flight · a compass rose  
**Sacred Creatures:** All migratory birds, particularly great albatrosses  
**Plane:** The Plane of Air  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Second Month Flowtide|Flowtide (2nd Month)]]

Deity of wind, change, freedom, and connection. Believes all living things are linked — every breath shared between strangers a form of communion. Followers are nomads, traders, diplomats, wanderers. The aarakocra feel particular kinship. Depicted as neither male nor female, always in motion, hair becoming cloud becoming horizon.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Aestri|Saint Aestri]]*

## Kindra — The Fervent Flame

![[assets/new-god-kindra.jpg]]

**Domains:** Fire, invention, the future, cleansing, rebirth through flame  
**Symbol:** A flame in an open palm · a gear wreathed in fire · twin burning eyes  
**Sacred Materials:** Worked metal, new mechanisms, ashes of written intention  
**Plane:** The Plane of Fire  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#First Month Ashwake|Ashwake (1st Month)]]

Deity of fire, invention, the future, and cleansing. The god who **looks forward** — the purpose of understanding the present is to build a better tomorrow. Their followers have produced the Isles' most significant technological advances and several of its most spectacular disasters.

The doctrine of **cleansing** is Kindra's most powerful and most abused teaching: the idea that fire can burn away sin, that a person who has done terrible things can be genuinely remade. In unscrupulous hands, this becomes the belief that any action can be cleansed afterward — a theology that has started at least two wars.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Tothiri|Saint Tothiri]]*  
*Dominant faith in: [[nations/Red Dragon Province|Red Province]], [[nations/Bronze Dragon Province|Bronze Province]]*

## Vapos — The Cleansing Tide

![[assets/new-god-vapos.jpg]]

**Domains:** Water, the past, healing, trade, recovery  
**Symbol:** Three concentric circles · a tide line · a vessel on calm water  
**Sacred Materials:** Clean water, healing herbs, ledgers of fair trade  
**Plane:** The Plane of Water  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Second Month Flowtide|Flowtide (2nd Month)]]

Goddess of water, the past, healing, and trade. Holds **three eyes** because she sees what was, what is, and what will be — but her interest is in the first of those. Wounds heal because water remembers what wholeness looked like. Trade flows because commerce is the accumulated memory of what people need from one another.

Followers run great hospitals, staff trading fleets, and serve as mediators in disputes between nations. The [[organisations/The Banks#TideLock Agreement|TideLock Agreement]] was negotiated almost entirely by Vapos's clergy.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Merrow|Saint Merrow]]*  
*Dominant faith in: [[nations/Black Dragon Province|Black Province]]*

## Neithona — The Oneiric Weaver

![[assets/new-god-neithona.jpg]]

**Domains:** Chaos, imagination, fate, dreams, the cycle of destruction and creation  
**Symbol:** A spider's web with a figure at its centre · a shifting kaleidoscope · a blank canvas  
**Sacred Creatures:** Spiders, moths, crows  
**Plane:** The Plane of Chaos  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Sixth Month Dreamfall|Dreamfall (6th Month)]]

Goddess of **free-will chaos** — not the destructive chaos of the Manic Blaze, but the chaos of imagination. Destiny, in her theology, is not what must happen, but what becomes possible when you are most fully yourself.

She sends dreams to all beings within the material plane. Some are beautiful. Some are terrible. All are meaningful, though the meanings are rarely comfortable. The cult of [[faith/Saints#Saint Ysolde|Saint Ysolde]] is the most intense expression of her faith.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Ysolde|Saint Ysolde]]*

## Shangotha — The Ankylosed Order

![[assets/new-god-shangotha.jpg]]

**Domains:** Order, law, civilisation, purpose, empire and its costs  
**Symbol:** Scales in perfect balance · a key · a symmetrical architectural arch  
**Sacred Materials:** Written law, city plans, records of civic life  
**Plane:** The Plane of Law  
**Month:** [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Eighth Month Goldend|Goldend (8th Month)]]

God of **order as civilisation** — law as the agreement between people that makes cooperative life possible. Patron of cities, courts, guilds, and institutions of every kind. The tension in Shangotha's faith is the same tension in every human institution: the difference between order that serves people and order that serves itself.

The [[organisations/The Chromatic Inquisition|Chromatic Inquisition]] operates in Shangotha's name, and has answered that question differently in different eras.

*Key Saint: [[faith/Saints#Saint Korden|Saint Korden]]*  
*Dominant faith in: [[nations/Blue Dragon Province|Blue Province]]*
