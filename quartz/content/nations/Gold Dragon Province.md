# Gold Dragon Province

#nations #dragon-province #gold-province

> *"Everything has a value. The wisdom is in understanding what that value actually is, rather than what someone is willing to perform."*

**Provincial Dragon:** Ancient Gold Dragon **Silvantis**  
**Capital:** Gildrathis  
**Economy:** Financial heart of the Fractured Isles; banking, hot spring resorts, gambling

---

## Overview

The financial heart of the Fractured Isles and its most uncomfortable mirror. Genuinely wealthy, genuinely beautiful, and genuinely committed to the idea that **the organisation of economic life is itself a form of wisdom**. Also the province that has most thoroughly built its public culture around the performance of wealth — with all of the anxiety and dishonesty that performance requires.

The hot springs produce a resort economy of considerable sophistication. The shadow side: a social culture in which the inability to maintain the appearance of prosperity is treated as moral failure, producing a population with access to extraordinary financial institutions and genuinely crushing levels of personal debt.

---

## Major Settlements

**Gildrathis** *(Capital)*  
The financial and resort capital of the Isles. The main banking citadel sits at the city's centre, surrounded by commercial and hospitality districts. Population ~250,000, with significant visitor numbers.

**Aurumgrad**  
The province's second city, primarily a financial processing centre with a more functional and less theatrical character than the capital.

![[province-gold-city.jpg]]

**Mertanthis**  
A resort city built entirely around three major hot spring complexes: the famous establishments **Gildai**, **Grand Ossirium**, and **Argentum**.

## Provincial Dragon: Silvantis

![[dragon-silvantis.jpg|assets/dragon-silvantis.jpg]]

**Humanoid Form:** Tall, elegant, with almost elven facial qualities, golden scales visible on the skin. Impeccably dressed at all times. Always in possession of at least one ledger.
**Greatest Achievement:** The promissory note system and the banking infrastructure it created. The Isles' economic stability for two thousand years.  
**Greatest Regret:** The social culture the province has developed around wealth performance. Silvantis finds it distasteful and has not been able to see change it.

**Current Plot Hooks:**
- A significant sum has been transferred out of a provincial account with authorisation codes that should not exist.
- A debt held by a smaller nation is being called in under unusual circumstances.
- Something is being financed through the banks that the transaction records have been altered to obscure.

## Provincial Festival
**Counting Day** (during [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Eighth Month Goldend|Goldend]]): Markets, auctions, and trade fairs. Economic predictions for the coming year presented publicly. Treated as entertainment by most residents and as scripture by a few.
