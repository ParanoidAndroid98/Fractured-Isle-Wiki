# Copper Dragon Province

#nations #dragon-province #copper-province

> *"The self is the first and most important creation. Everything else follows from the quality of that work."*

**Provincial Dragon:** Ancient Copper Dragon **Dask-Ijer**  
**Capital:** Dizaniss  
**Economy:** Art, performance, finest armour and weaponry in the Isles

---

## Overview

The cultural capital of the Fractured Isles — the best art, the most celebrated performances, the most technically refined armour and weaponry all trace their lineage here. Also the province with the **most visible class divide**, and with the most active and complicated discourse about what that divide means.

The arts here are not decorative. They are the **primary language through which the province processes everything that happens to it**. Political events become performances within months. Social tensions become artistic movements. The armourers and weaponsmiths are considered artists, and the weapons they produce are displayed in exhibitions alongside paintings and sculpture.

## Major Settlements

![[province-copper-city.jpg]]

**Dizaniss** *(Capital)*  
A city that changes its face seasonally — performance banners replaced, public sculptures rotated, facades recoloured. Navigating it confidently is considered a mark of genuine familiarity. Population ~170,000.

**Cupralis**  
The province's craft manufacturing centre. More prosperous than it looks; the wealth here is invested back into the craft.

**Tasarnisforge**  
The province's specialist forge city, producing the most technically refined worked metal objects in the Isles. Long waiting lists for commissions.

## Provincial Dragon: Dask-Ijer

![[assets/dragon-dask-ijer.jpg]]

**Humanoid Form:** Human-featured with draconic wings maintained in humanoid form — unusual and deliberate. Playful androgynous features, elaborate clothing and makeup that shift colours and patterns as they speak. Rarely the same appearance twice.

**Greatest Achievement:** The Verdisicule Institute's open workshop programme, making the province's craft training accessible to those without the means to pay full tuition.  
**Greatest Regret:** The class divide that the province's economic model produces. Dask-Ijer has not solved it. They are aware that they have not solved it.

**Current Plot Hooks:**
- A commission has been placed for a weapon with specifications that the armourers' guild has referred to the Inquisition.
- A performance has been running in Dizaniss for six months with increasing audience devotion that looks, to outside observers, like something more than artistic appreciation.
- The class divide is approaching a political tipping point.

## Provincial Festival
**Grand Masquerade** (during [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Sixth Month Dreamfall|Dreamfall]]): The most famous festival in the Isles. Week-long event involving art, music, theatre, and costuming of extraordinary ambition. Provincial attendance swells threefold.
