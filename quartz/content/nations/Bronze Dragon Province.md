> *"Knowledge is not dangerous. Ignorance is dangerous. The answer to dangerous knowledge is not less knowledge; it is more people who understand it well enough to handle it."*

**Capital:** Avioln Arcan  
**Dominant Faith:** [[cosmology/The New Gods#Kindra|Kindra]], [[faith/Saints#Saint Tothiri|Tothiri]]

---

## Overview

The closest thing the Fractured Isles has to a magical theme park — if theme parks routinely had sections cordoned off because the last experiment had not quite finished resolving. Also home to the **most significant concentration of genuine arcane research** in the world, the most innovative teleportation network (currently offline pending investigation), and the most welcoming attitude toward anyone who wants to learn regardless of where they come from.

The provincial motto, unofficially, is **"we will try it and see."**

---

## Government

A **sorcerer's democracy** — citizens with demonstrated magical ability vote on provincial policy, with weight proportional to their assessed skill level. Citizens without magical ability can earn voting rights through academic qualification. This produces a government that is technically meritocratic, almost entirely composed of academics and researchers, and occasionally wildly impractical in its approaches to problems that do not yield to experimental methodology.

## Major Settlements

![[province-bronze-city.jpg]]

**Avioln Arcan** *(Capital)*  
"The place of active arcane." The city is built vertically as much as horizontally, with the Aurumspire Institute occupying the highest towers. Population ~220,000.

**Sablebrace**  
The province's primary industrial application centre, where theoretical research from the capital is tested at scale. Visitors are advised to pay attention to the colour-coded safety flags.

**Thalerix Spire**  
A research city focused specifically on spatial magic — teleportation, dimensional study, and the investigation of planar boundaries. Currently maintaining an **elevated caution status**.

## Provincial Dragon: Fiztril

![[assets/dragon-fiztril.jpg]]

**Humanoid Form:** A great old gnomish-featured sorcerer in long flowing robes, bronze scales visible on the sides of the face and temples. Luminous white eyes behind thick-lensed spectacles. Carries several books at all times.

**Personality:** Intellectually voracious, deeply kind in a slightly distracted way, occasionally forgets that the people around them are not primarily interested in the same problems they are.  
**Greatest Achievement:** The Aurumspire curriculum — a model of arcane education that has been adapted by institutions across the Isles.  
**Greatest Regret:** Approving the Thalerix Spire spatial research programme without the oversight structures it clearly required.

**Current Plot Hooks:**
- Something is happening at Thalerix Spire that the province is not publicly explaining.
- Fiztril has been spending unusual time in consultation with the Ebony Order.
- A research assistant has not been seen for three weeks and their colleagues have been asked not to discuss their work.

## Provincial Festival
**Arcane Symposium** (during [[faith/Calendar and Festivals#Fifth Month Starveil|Starveil]]): Every major magical institution sends delegates. Described by attendees as a mixture of academic conference and magical disaster in approximately equal proportion.
