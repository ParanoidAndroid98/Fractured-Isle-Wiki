# Organisations of the Fractured Isles

#organisations

The institutions of the Fractured Isles were each built in the shadow of the catastrophes that preceded them. Every organisation here was built by someone who believed they were doing the right thing — and most of them were right, at least in part.

## Relationships Between Organisations

The **Ebony Order** and the **Arcane Senate** are the defining institutional tension of the magical world. The Senate believes knowledge should flow as freely as safety allows; the Order believes safety should be more conservative than the Senate tends to think. Both positions have been validated and catastrophically wrong at different points in history.

The **Chromatic Inquisition** works closely with the Ebony Order and is often mistaken for a branch of it. It is not. The Order concerns itself with knowledge and artifacts; the Inquisition concerns itself with people and power. In practice, the two organisations share information, personnel, and occasionally operational authority in ways that blur the distinction considerably.

The **Banks** are technically separate from political power — and in practice cannot be entirely distinguished from it.

The **Pathfinders** maintain political neutrality as a core institutional value and have done so consistently enough over 1,200 years that even the Inquisition generally takes their geographic records at face value.
